### FAQ Section

**What's the difference between rough and finish grading?**
Rough grading shapes the overall terrain and drainage. Finish grading is the final, precise layer that prepares the surface for foundations, paving, or landscaping.

**Do you handle drainage grading?**
Yes. Proper drainage is critical in the Scottsdale desert environment. We grade to your drainage plans to prevent water issues.

**Can you grade to engineered specifications?**
Yes. We work from civil engineering plans and grade to exact elevations and tolerances required.

**Do you coordinate compaction testing?**
We coordinate with third-party testing firms to ensure all compaction meets project specifications.

### FAQ Section

**What types of retaining walls do you build?**
We build CMU block walls, segmental retaining walls, poured concrete walls, and tiered/terraced wall systems.

**Do retaining walls need engineering?**
In most cases, yes. Walls over 4 feet typically require engineered plans. We work with structural engineers to ensure compliance.

**How long does a retaining wall project take?**
Most residential retaining walls take 1-2 weeks. Commercial projects vary based on size, height, and complexity.

**Do you handle drainage behind the wall?**
Yes. Proper drainage and backfill are critical to wall performance. We install drainage systems as part of every retaining wall project.
